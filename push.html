Push Day Explained

I'm a strong believer in Pull Ups, a great excercise meant to warm the upper body up. Just want you to 3 reps where you feel the contractions in your back. Next is my favorite lift, Dumbell Bench Press. Here you have to retract your shoulder blades and angle your arms at a 45 degree angle. We want to go HEAVY, and progressively increase the weight we push. Thirdly we will target our sholders with Lateral Raises, built shoulders make you appear more built. Lastly we'll finish off our push day with Cable Chest Flys to really sqeeze our chest and pump as much blood into the area.
