Leg Day Explained

Leg Day, the most dreaded day of all. Here we will begin the workout with heavy Barbell Squats, a perfect full body excercise where we will primarily target your quads and hamstrings. Depth is a big aspect of this list, to perform a propper squat you must squat low enough where your thighs are parallel to the floor. Next we will isolate our hamstrings using the Hamstrings Machine stretching and contracting on each rep. Another isometric excercise is the Leg Extension Machine where the quads are the main focus of the lift, meant to shape and tone the quads. To end the workout we will do a series of Calf Raises to help make your legs more proportional.

Exercise Name Sets Repetitions Intensity
Barbell Squat 5 4 Heavy
Hamstring Machine 3 10 Medium
Leg Extension Machine 3 10 Medium
Calf Raises 3 10 Medium
